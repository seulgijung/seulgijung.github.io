Hello, I am a freelance data journalist from South Korea. I was brought to a world of reporting with numbers and graphic by The LEDE program, a data journalism course for professionals at Columbia University.

Before coming to journalism, I worked at a civil engineering & construction company, managing ongoing projects and collecting and refining client based data. I also did a one year intership at MinKwon Center for Community Action, a non-profit organization in NYC serving Korean immigrants as well as other immigrants and advocationg civil rights.

I am passionate to cover social issues crossing South Korea and the U.S. and am especially interested in immigrant issue. I am currently volunteering for Korean American Peace Fund, whose website and social media accounts I helped create & run.

< class="accordion">Korean Chinese: Once Compatriots, Now Targeted of Xenophobia

I covered a soaring xenophobic phenomenon in Korea, singularly targeting Korean Chinese, called joseon-jok. I used Selenium to scrape data and Python to refine it. The graph was created with free Javascript source, and ai2html & Scrollama were used for the other graphic Korean translation is available for this piece.

Is North Korea Preparing for a War?

I got a hint for this project from a presentaion by Mr. Suh Jae-jeong, a professor in the Department of Politics and International Studies at International Christian University, clearing the North's true intention amid the current security crisis on the Korean peninsula. I used Mapbox Scrollytelling template and added some structural changes using Javascript. Satelite images were gained from Google Earth.
